Features and Specifications

Cherry MX switches allow for extremely precise inputs of 3ms, a satisfying “clicky” tactile feedback, and extended durability of up to three million key presses!

Adaptive LEDs respond to a library of over 3000 games to provide additional depth to your experience. DGaming’s signature LEDs can display up to 18.6 billion colors!

Textured keycaps provide more grip on your fingertips to provide a more comfortable and consistent gaming experience. Additional interchangeable keycaps are also provided!

Additional function keys can be reprogrammed through the DGaming Suite to execute custom user-created macros. Each of the nine function keys can also have different uses for supported games!

About the DG Mechanical Ultra

Since the creation of mechanical keyboards, video games have pushed a single request upon the industry: a sleek, tactile keyboard that balances comfort during long term usage with the ultra-low response times needed for higher level play. While this feat was thought to have been impossible for the greater part of the decade, our team has finally developed what we believe to be the forefront of the industry. The DG Mechanical Ultra not only achieves these goals with ease, but it surpasses the expectations of nearly all who are ready to use it.

The DG Mechanical Ultra boasts the following specifications:

  • 3 millisecond response time upon keydown
  • Interchangeable Cherry MX switches
  • LEDs with 18.6 billion color range
  • Colorful LEDs that interact with a library of 3000+ games
  • Interchangeable keycaps with high durability and a textile feel
  • 10 remappable function keys
  • 8 ft ultra speed cable
  • Comfortable padding for resting wrists
  • Full compatibility with the DGaming Suite software
